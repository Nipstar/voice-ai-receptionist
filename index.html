Sound Familiar?

These problems cost UK businesses money every day

Whether you run a single-room therapy clinic, a high-street law firm, a busy dental surgery or a multi-van trades business — the same three problems drain your revenue and steal your time. An AI receptionist fixes all three.

Missed Calls = Lost Customers

You're with a client, in a treatment room, in court, in a meeting — and the phone rings. By the time you call back, the caller has booked your competitor. Around one in three UK businesses miss incoming calls, and roughly 69% of those callers never leave a voicemail. Every unanswered ring is revenue walking away.

Evenings and Weekends Lost to Admin

After a full day of appointments, jobs or client work, the last thing you want is to spend the evening returning missed calls, typing up quotes and chasing voicemails. But if you don't, tomorrow's diary stays empty. You didn't start your own business to live at a desk — yet the admin never stops.

Can't Justify a £22k-£28k Receptionist

A full-time UK receptionist costs £22,000-£28,000 a year plus NI, holiday, sickness and cover. For most small practices and SMEs that is huge overhead for someone who only needs to be on the phone a few hours a day. An AI receptionist covers the same work 24/7 for a fraction of the cost.
